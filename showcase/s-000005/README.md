# ANICA Core Overload / Logo Reactor Explosion 10s

A high-impact, scene + post-process MotionLoom DSL demo that turns the ANICA logo into a charging reactor core, triggers a radial explosion, and reforms the logo into a GPU/WebGPU badge.

Use in Anica:

1. Open the MotionLoom / VFX page.
2. Open `main.motionloom`.
3. Paste the full DSL into the MotionLoom editor.
4. Preview or render.

## Sequence breakdown

| Time | Section | What happens |
|------|---------|--------------|
| 0.0–1.5s | HUD Scan | ANICA logo fades in with glitch offsets, HUD brackets, scanlines, and data readouts. |
| 1.5–3.5s | Charge Up | Multiple rings spin and scale inward, core glow intensifies, bloom rises, warm/cyan HSLA tint builds. |
| 3.5–4.5s | Explosion | White flash, expanding shockwave ring, radial rays, square fragments, and streak lines burst outward. |
| 4.5–7.0s | Smoke / Dust | Semi-transparent smoke blobs drift outward while gaussian blur increases for a hazy aftermath. |
| 7.0–10.0s | Reformation | Particles converge, ANICA logo reforms, "MotionLoom Graph DSL" and "GPU / WebGPU" badges appear. |

## MotionLoom features demonstrated

- **Scene graph** with multiple `Track` layers and `z` depth sorting.
- **Curve animation** via `curve("time:value:easing, ...")` on position, scale, rotation, opacity, radius, and stroke width.
- **Repeat** for particle rings, scanlines, rays, fragments, and smoke blobs.
- **Group** transforms with `rotationStep`, `scale`, and `transformOrigin`.
- **Primitives**: `Text`, `Rect`, `Circle`, `Line`, `Group`.
- **Blend modes**: `screen` for glows, blooms, and light effects.
- **Defs**: `RadialGradient`, `LinearGradient`, and reusable `Font` resources.
- **Post-process pipeline**:
  - `hsla_overlay` for animated color grading.
  - `glow_bloom` for energy bloom and flash glow.
  - `gaussian_5tap_blur` for smoke/dust softness.
- **GPU/WebGPU ready**: rendered through the `Process` + `Present` pipeline.
